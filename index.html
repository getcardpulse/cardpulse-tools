01
Overvalued

Is this card riding hype or real demand?

We separate organic growth (steady sales, thinning supply, durable character premium) from manipulated growth (buyout spikes, momentum chasing, mania-phase pricing). One survives corrections. The other doesn't.

Jungle Pikachu PSA 10: $1K in 2020 mania → crashed to $200 → back at $1K now. 3,000+ PSA 10s exist. We'd flag this as hype-driven.
02
Undervalued

Which cards should be worth more than they are?

Pull difficulty, character premium, grading economics, and cultural relevance create a "fair value" baseline. Cards trading significantly below it, especially with a catalyst approaching, are where the real opportunities are.

Dragonite ex #290 (Ascended Heroes): High pull difficulty, first premium Dragonite printing since Evolving Skies, strong character premium. Demand still catching up to fundamentals.
03
Timing

What's about to change, and when?

Set rotations, print cycle endings, franchise anniversaries, Netflix seasons, license shifts, grading velocity spikes. These are the catalysts that move card prices. We track them so you can act before the market catches on.
